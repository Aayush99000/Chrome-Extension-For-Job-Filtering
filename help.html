üöÄ Getting Started

  1. Upload Your Resume: Click "Choose File" and select your PDF or DOCX resume
  2. Process: Click "Process Resume" to analyze your skills and experience
  3. Browse Jobs: Visit LinkedIn, Indeed, or Glassdoor job pages
  4. See Matches: Each job will automatically show a match score
  5. Apply Wisely: Focus on high-match jobs (70%+)

üìä Understanding Match Scores

High Match (70-100%): Strong alignment with your skills and experience. Good fit!

Medium Match (40-69%): Partial alignment. Review job details carefully.

Low Match (0-39%): Limited alignment. May require additional skills.

Match scores are based on semantic similarity between your resume and job descriptions, not just keyword matching.

üí° Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why isn't the extension working on a job site?
A: Make sure you've uploaded your resume first. Then try clicking the "Scan Current Page" button manually. The extension works on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor.
Q: Do I need to re-upload my resume each time?
A: No! Your resume is stored locally and persists across sessions. You only need to upload it once (or when you want to update it).
Q: Is my resume data secure?
A: Yes! Your resume is stored locally in your browser and processed on a local backend. It's never sent to external servers.
Q: Why are some match scores surprising?
A: The AI uses semantic understanding, not just keywords. "Backend Engineer" might match well with "Server Developer" even though the words differ. This helps you discover relevant opportunities you might have missed.
Q: How do I change my settings?
A: Click the "Settings" link at the bottom of the main popup to customize match thresholds, display preferences, and more.
Q: The backend isn't running. What do I do?
A: The extension requires a local Python backend to be running. Open your terminal, navigate to the backend folder, and run: python app.py

üîß Troubleshooting

  • Extension not showing scores: Check that the backend is running (python app.py)
  • Upload failed: Ensure your file is PDF or DOCX format
  • Scores not updating: Try refreshing the job page or clicking "Scan Current Page"
  • Wrong scores: Try re-uploading an updated resume
